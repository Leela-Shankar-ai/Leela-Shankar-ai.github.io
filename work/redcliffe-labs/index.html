HEALTH DIAGNOSTICS · PILOT AD FILM

Redcliffe Labs

The situation

One of India’s biggest diagnostics companies. Their brand ambassador is one of India’s best known cricketers, and they wanted a new campaign film without scheduling a shoot with him.

What we delivered

  • A pilot ad film made without the ambassador ever coming on set
  • Our founder performed the ad himself on camera: script, gestures, expressions and voice
  • Motion capture transferred every movement and gesture onto the ambassador
  • The voice replaced with the ambassador’s, matched to the new performance
  • Delivered as a 30 second AI face scan campaign film with a QR call to action

Why it worked

The ambassador’s calendar stopped being the bottleneck. The founder’s performance became his.
