Scientific Research Fact Check Pipeline

The situation

Health videos on YouTube make big claims and almost nobody checks the papers behind them. Leela wanted a system that reads the actual science, so he built an AI peer reviewer.

What we delivered

  • A pipeline that downloads a video, transcribes it, and finds every research paper it cites
  • Reads the full papers and cross references every claim against what they actually say
  • Puts every paper through a 9 point scientific integrity check: was it randomized, placebo controlled, double blind, peer reviewed, replicable and statistically significant, and are the authors and funders free of conflicts of interest
  • Produces a complete written report, and once produced a full rebuttal video script with 22 verified citations

Why it worked

The same evidence first discipline now runs inside our client research work. We verify, we do not vibe.
