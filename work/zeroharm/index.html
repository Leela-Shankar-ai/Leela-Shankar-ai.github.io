The situation

A D2C nutraceutical brand selling supplements across marketplaces and Meta ads. Creative decisions lived in people’s heads, and every new ad started from a blank page.

What we delivered

  • ZeroHarm OS: an operating system for their entire creative organization
  • A knowledge base covering the full catalog. For every product: why it exists, the target audience, the FAQs, the scientific facts, the buyer archetypes, the funnel, the working structure and what is not working in its ads
  • A Script Validator that scores every new ad script, predicts its Meta performance and expected ROAS, and gives a clear go or no go
  • Competitor intelligence that finds rival listings on Google Shopping, reads their Meta ads and how they perform, then recreates the winners with AI
  • Weekly automated research runs, so the system keeps learning from live ad spend
