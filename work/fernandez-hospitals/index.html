WOMEN & CHILDREN’S HOSPITALS · HYDERABAD

Fernandez Hospitals

The situation

One of Hyderabad’s most trusted women and children’s hospital groups. Patient conversations happen in public across four platforms, and rival hospitals publish content every week, so the team was reading everything by hand.

What we delivered

  • A social listening dashboard pulling YouTube, Instagram, Facebook and Google Reviews into one screen
  • Comment to post jumping, so every remark is read in its full context
  • A blog intelligence tool that maps rival hospitals’ publishing into a searchable, AI enriched dataset
  • Every article tagged for tone, language and keywords across English and Telugu

Why it worked

The patient experience team reads everything in minutes instead of platform hopping, and the content team writes with the whole city’s publishing in view.
