Agentic AI Video Editing

The situation

An experiment with a simple question: can an AI agent edit a video by itself, from the command line, with taste? It can.

What we delivered

  • A raw talking head video goes in, and the agent transcribes it and plans the edit
  • It builds animated motion graphic scenes as code: title cards, rank cards, stamps and a progress bar
  • A 500 line style guide teaches it our editing taste, with 6 scene archetypes
  • It renders a finished vertical reel with no human touching the timeline

Why it worked

Teach the machine your editing taste once and it edits alone. This experiment is becoming a Media Lab service.
